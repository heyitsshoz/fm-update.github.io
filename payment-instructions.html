Payment Instructions

Please follow these steps carefully to complete your registration fee payment.

Step 1
Find the Institution

Visit the ICICI eazypay portal and search for KREA UNIVERSITY PROGRAM FEE in the institution name field.

Step 2
Fill the form

Provide a valid mobile number to receive the required OTP via text message. Enter FMU26 in both the 'Program Name' and 'Course' fields. Enter the fee amount that corresponds to your expected number of days of stay. Please note that all fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.

Step 3
Complete Payment

On the summary page, ensure you deselect"Other Fees". Choose your preferred payment method (Net Banking, UPI, or Card) and complete the transaction.

Step 4
Keep the transaction id handy

Kindly keep your transaction id handy, just incase of any issues that may arise due to technical glitches
